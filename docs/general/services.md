# SeesamiData Consulting — Services & About

## Who We Are
SeesamiData Consulting is a boutique data science and AI consulting practice
founded by Mai Nguyen — PhD in Integrative Life Sciences, bioinformatician,
and former data scientist at BCPlatforms. Based in Helsinki, working remotely
across Europe.

The name reflects the philosophy: like the sesame seed — small, ancient, full
of hidden value. Every locked data problem has its sesame. We find yours.

DataReady is the AI-powered assessment tool built by SeesamiData — it automates
the first analytical step of a consulting engagement, turning a plain-language
problem statement into a structured data readiness brief.

Mai holds a PhD in Computational Biology (University of Helsinki) with a thesis
on computational analysis of circulating tumour DNA in ovarian cancer, an MSc in
Bioinformatics (Hasselt University, Belgium), and a BBA in Corporate Finance
(University of Economics, HCMC). This rare combination of deep domain science,
data engineering, and business background is what SeesamiData brings to every
engagement.

Prior to founding SeesamiData, Mai worked as a Data Scientist and Project
Coordinator at BCPlatforms (Jan 2023 – Jun 2025), running client-facing discovery
workshops, designing and implementing ETL pipelines for clinical data using AWS,
Airflow, and WDL, transforming large-scale real-world datasets into OMOP and other
standardised models for regulatory submissions and registry-based research, and
conducting data readiness and feasibility assessments for real-world data and AI
use cases across biopharma clients.

## Core Services

### 1. Data Readiness Assessment
A structured evaluation of your organisation's data maturity across five dimensions:
data quality, governance, infrastructure, people & process, and analytics/AI readiness.
Delivered as a consulting brief with readiness score, capability gaps, governance
flags, and prioritised next steps. Powered by the DataReady AI tool.

Typical engagement: 1–2 weeks
Best for: organisations considering an AI initiative, regulatory submission, or data
platform investment who need to understand where they actually stand before committing.

### 2. Data Strategy & Roadmap
A 90-day or 12-month data strategy tailored to your business context. Covers target
architecture, governance model, team capability requirements, tooling recommendations,
and a phased delivery plan with milestones and owners.

Typical engagement: 2–4 weeks
Best for: scale-ups and mid-size organisations that have accumulated data debt and
need a clear, prioritised path forward.

### 3. Governance Framework Setup
Design and implementation of a practical data governance framework aligned to
DAMA-DMBOK. Includes data ownership model, data quality standards, metadata
management, glossary, and policy documentation. Pragmatic and proportionate to
your organisation's size — not an enterprise framework forced onto a small team.

Typical engagement: 4–8 weeks
Best for: organisations preparing for regulatory audit, data sharing agreements,
or investor due diligence who need demonstrable governance in place.

### 4. AI/ML Readiness & Implementation
A focused assessment of whether your data, infrastructure, and team are ready to
support a specific AI or ML use case — followed by end-to-end implementation if
needed. Covers data availability and quality for model training, infrastructure
readiness, model risk considerations, SHAP-based explainability, FastAPI serving,
and production deployment.

Example delivered: end-to-end clinical risk prediction platform — FHIR R4
harmonisation pipeline into Gradient Boosting models achieving AUC 0.971 for
cancer risk prediction, served via FastAPI with Power BI integration.

Typical engagement: 2–6 weeks depending on scope
Best for: organisations evaluating an AI investment or ready to move from proof of
concept to a production-grade system.

### 5. Real-World Evidence & Bioinformatics Pipelines
Design and implementation of scalable data pipelines for EHR, registry, and claims
data. OMOP CDM transformation, data quality frameworks, and statistical analysis
for regulatory submissions. End-to-end bioinformatics pipeline engineering for
single-cell RNA-seq, multiomics, ctDNA liquid biopsy, CNV detection, and somatic
mutation calling.

Typical engagement: 4–12 weeks
Best for: biopharma and life science organisations building or scaling clinical
data infrastructure, RWE capabilities, or genomics pipelines.
